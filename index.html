02

Medical Imaging · Deep Learning · Research

TempVSNet — Brain Tumor Growth Prediction

Designed TempVSNet, a dual-encoder 3D ConvLSTM model that predicts future Vestibular Schwannoma tumor masks from MRI triplets. Trained on 147 triplets across 80 patients with 5-fold cross-validation. Sinusoidal time encoding + compound Dice + Focal + Boundary loss. Deployed as a Flask-based Clinical Decision Support System with role-based login and PDF reporting. Journal paper in preparation.

Dice 0.827 HD95 1.88mm AUC-ROC 0.9497 80 patients
Python PyTorch MONAI ANTsPy Flask 3D U-Net
